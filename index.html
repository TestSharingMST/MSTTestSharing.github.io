MST Test Sharing

Welcome to the MST Test Sharing website! Our Goal is to make a website that allows all current and previous students to share tests amongst one another. Feel free to download any test or quiz you feel like. Using Ctrl + F will allow you to quickly search for the class and test years will be listed below. If you have a test you would like to submit for addition to the site please email us at __________ with the subject as the class code, year and semester of test(ex. MATH 2222 2017 Fall). Also, please make sure that the file is in PDF. There is a free app that allows you to do this called CamScanner. MST Test Sharing is in no way affiliated with any part of the University of Missouri system or parts of the Missouri University of Science and Technology. Under Missouri University of Science and Technology's Student Academic Regulations Chapter XI Part B.1.a.(iii). cheating is referred to as "acquisition or possession without permission of tests or other academic material belonging to a member of the University faculty or staff". With submission to the website the sender is acknowledging that if the provided test or quiz is not allowed to be released that they are held responsible. All content on this site is not our property and is the property of the creator of said test or quiz. If any creator of a test would like their respective test removed from the site please contact us using a verifiable email at __________ with proof of creation.
